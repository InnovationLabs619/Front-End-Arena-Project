Our Story

What started as a humble cloud kitchen in the heart of Hyderabad has now blossomed into a full-fledged restaurant, proudly known as Ancient Express. From the very beginning, our mission has been simple yet powerful: to preserve the soul of traditional cooking and share timeless flavors with the world.


Our recipes aren’t just culinary instructions — they’re heirlooms passed down through generations. Each dish on our menu has its roots in a grandmother’s kitchen somewhere across the globe. Whether it's the spice-laden biryanis of South India, a European comfort stew, or a delicately balanced Asian broth, every bite carries the warmth of home and the wisdom of age-old traditions.


At Ancient Express, we stay true to that legacy by sourcing the freshest local spices and globally loved ingredients. Our chefs bring passion, patience, and precision to every dish, ensuring you experience authentic flavors just the way they were meant to be — honest, soulful, and made with love.


We invite you to dine with us not just for a meal, but for a journey through cultures, stories, and memories — all served on a plate.
